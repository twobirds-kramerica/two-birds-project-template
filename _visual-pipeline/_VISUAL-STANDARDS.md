# Visual Standards
# Two Birds Projects | Aaron Kramer

---

## Brand Identity — Two Birds

Two Birds is a digital services business helping people
(especially seniors) gain confidence with technology.

Visual identity principles:
- Warm, trustworthy, professional
- Human-centred — people first, not technology first
- Clean and uncluttered — nothing overwhelming
- Accessible — high contrast, legible typography

## Typography

### Digital Confidence Centre
Headings: Georgia or similar serif — conveys trust and readability
Body: System sans-serif stack — clean and accessible
Minimum body size: 16px
Line height: 1.6 minimum

### Kirks Karate
Headings: Strong sans-serif (bold)
Body: Clean sans-serif
Minimum body size: 16px

---

## Photography Standards

### Must Have
- Real-looking people (not obviously AI-generated stock)
- Genuine expressions
- Appropriate diversity
- Good lighting — warm and natural preferred

### Must Not Have
- Condescending portrayals of seniors (confused, struggling)
- Aggressive or violent imagery (even for karate)
- Cluttered or busy backgrounds
- Low resolution or blurry images

---

## Accessibility

All images must have descriptive alt text.
Decorative images use empty alt="" attribute.
Text overlaid on images must meet WCAG AA contrast ratio.
